### 🎧 Lossless Audio Streaming
Enjoy high-quality audio playback with optimized streaming performance.

- Lossless audio support
- Smooth playback experience
- Smart buffering
- High bitrate audio delivery

---

### 📥 Offline Downloads

Download your favorite tracks directly to your device and listen anytime.

- High-bitrate MP3 extraction
- Offline playback
- Smart cache management
- Download manager

Keep your music library synchronized across devices.

- Real-time Firebase synchronization
- Playlist backup
- Favorites synchronization
- Cross-device library access
- Instant cloud restore

### 🚫 Ad-Free Experience

Enjoy uninterrupted music.

- Ad detection system
- Automatic mute & skip
- Background playback support
- Clean listening experience

Never stop the flow.

- Intelligent autoplay
- Real-time recommendations
- Crossfade transitions
- Continuous playback
